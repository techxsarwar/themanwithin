Meet the Author

Faisal Rasool

Student, Writer, Dreamer

A student of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, born in Anantnag and hailing from D.H. Pora, Kulgam, in Jammu & Kashmir.

At just 15 years old, Faisal has embarked on a journey to explore the profound depths of human emotion. He writes with a rare honesty and an intuitive understanding of the silent battles fought within the mind.

Fascinated by human nature, he seeks to give a voice to feelings that often remain unspoken in our fast-paced world.

"The Man Within" is his debut book—an honest exploration of the inner world and a testament to the power of reflection.
